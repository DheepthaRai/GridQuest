Optimization Objective

The app is minimizing the weighted distance from each demand point to its closest facility.

Minimize: sum over all demand points of (demand value × distance to nearest facility)

How It Calculates

⌄
  • Step 1: For every demand point, find the closest facility.
  • Step 2: Respect capacity, so a facility can only absorb demand up to the chosen limit.
  • Step 3: Compute the selected objective using the served assignments.
  • For `1 facility`, the app checks every integer grid point from `(0,0)` to `(50,50)`.
  • For `3 facilities`, the app checks every triple of demand-point candidate locations and picks the best total score.

How to Use It

⌄
  • Pick `1 facility` or `3 facilities` from the control panel.
  • Drag a facility marker directly, or select a facility in the list and click the board to reposition it.
  • Every demand point is scored against its closest facility, weighted by its demand value.
  • `Show Optimal` reveals the best 1-site layout, or the best 3-site layout over all demand-point candidate sites.
